Batangas is a province located in the Calabarzon region of the Philippines. It is known for its beautiful beaches and diving spots, such as Anilao and Mabini. The province is also famous for its Taal Volcano, which is one of the most active volcanoes in the country. Batangas is also known for its rich cultural heritage and is home to many historical landmarks, such as the Taal Basilica and the Maliputo Lighthouse. The province is also known for its delicious local cuisine, such as the Batangas lomi and the kakanin desserts.
